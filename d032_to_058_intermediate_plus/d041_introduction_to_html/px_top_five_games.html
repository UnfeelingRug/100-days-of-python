Damien's Favourite Games

My Top 5 Games of All Time

Super Mario Sunshine

This one is a no-brainer - it's my first game I ever played, and I have great memories of it.

Outer Wilds

I love the aesthetic, the different planets, how deep the exploration goes, the implementation of the time-loop, and how you always have something new to investigate.

The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe

A great expansion on the original, with a hilarious narrator and a wide range of ways to find new content and story paths.

XCOM 2: War of the Chosen

Challenging, strategic gameplay with a lot of character customization and upgrade options; fun enemies to fight, cool missions, and it feels really satisfying to get a good plan together.

Monster Hunter World

Honestly just a really solid game - great to play with friends, tons of gameplay options whether you're a sword user, bonkmaster, dootstick enjoyer, anything. Tons of challenge, tons of variety.
